How It Works
Dumb Wire, Running All Winter
Heat cable on roofs runs 24/7 — no sensors, no monitoring, nobody watching. Wasted energy. $35k+/year on large commercial and multifamily properties.
PowderWatts Installs Smart Switch + Sensor
AI watches the roof. Cable only runs when it needs to. 24/7 monitoring catches failures before they cause damage. The utility writes a check for most of the install.
Building Saves 80%+. Every Winter.
Payback in months. After that it's pure savings — the building just stops bleeding electricity. Every winter, permanently.
No Real Competition

"Aren't there already controllers for this?"

Temperature and snow-sensor systems exist. They're ineffective — they react to weather, not the roof. They can't tell you if a breaker tripped, a cable failed, or the system ran all night for no reason. PowderWatts watches what's actually happening.

There's no real equivalent on the market. Two issued US patents — with a third pending — make sure it stays that way.
